Introduction to the Internet

What is the Internet?

The Internet is a global network of computers that are connected to each other, allowing people to share information and communicate across the world. It works by sending data in small packets through cables and wireless connections to reach different devices. Websites, emails, videos, and apps all use the Internet to function. People use it for a wide variety of activities, from learning and working to entertainment and socializing. Essentially, it's a huge system that helps us stay connected and find information quickly.
